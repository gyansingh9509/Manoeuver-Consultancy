About Manoeuver Consultancy Services Pvt. Ltd.

Manoeuver Consultancy Services Pvt. Ltd. is a leading organization dedicated to empowering individuals and organizations through innovative consulting, training, and strategic solutions. Established in 2018, Manoeuver has been at the forefront of skill development, business transformation, and professional growth across India. Our commitment is to deliver measurable impact and sustainable success for our clients and partners.

Our Vision

To be a catalyst for positive change by enabling individuals and organizations to realize their full potential. We envision a future where skill, knowledge, and innovation drive inclusive growth and prosperity for all.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide expert consulting, impactful training, and tailored solutions that empower our clients to overcome challenges, embrace opportunities, and achieve excellence. We strive to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity through quality education, industry partnerships, and a commitment to lifelong learning.

Manoeuver Consultancy Services Pvt. Ltd. is recognized as a startup by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, Government of India. Our team of certified professionals and industry experts is dedicated to delivering value-driven services in human resources, recruitment, and skill development sectors. We are proud to be an authorized training partner with leading national and state-level organizations.

Major Areas of Work

  • Skill Development & Training – Empowering youth and professionals with industry-relevant skills.
  • Business Consulting – Providing strategic guidance for organizational growth and transformation.
  • Employment & Career Opportunities – Bridging the gap between talent and employers.
  • Social Impact Initiatives – Promoting health, education, and environmental awareness.
  • Industry Collaboration – Building partnerships for sustainable development and innovation.

Certificate of Recognition

Manoeuver Consultancy Services Pvt. Ltd. is proud to be recognized as a startup by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, Government of India. This certificate acknowledges our contribution to the Human Resources and Recruitment sector and our commitment to excellence and innovation.

About the Company

Manhit Exim Pvt. Ltd. is established since 2018, by Mr.Vikas Nagar (Director) of this organization. MEPL was started with the objective of offering multi-disciplinary learning management and training delivery solutions to corporations, institutions, and individuals in over 15-18 states.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

MEPL is imparting education and works to create and sustain an environment conducive to the development of India, partnering industry, government, and civil society, through advisory and consultative processes.

Our Mission

We are running the skill development courses under the various schemes of different ministries such as UPSDM, NSDC , SSC, RSLDC, ASDM, NULM We work on skills, trained and assist our students in finding suitable job opportunities for a better future.

MEPL have award winning team of professional certified Trainers, Graphic Designers, IT Experts who have 5+years experience in IT Industry. Since 5+ years success, conducting various government training and skill development programs. We are ATHORIZED TRAINING PARTNER WITH NATIONAL SKILL DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION (NSDC).

Major Areas of Work

  • Health Awareness
  • Rural Educational Programs & Campaign
  • Environmental Programs
  • Cultural Activities and Scholarship Programs
  • Employment & Career Opportunity

Awards Winning

We are proud to have been honored at the 2025 Conclave Awards for our outstanding contribution to skill development and employment generation. This recognition reflects our continued commitment to quality training, industry collaboration, and empowering individuals for a better future.

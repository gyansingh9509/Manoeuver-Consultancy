Skill Development Programs

Manoeuver Consultancy Services Pvt. Ltd. offers a wide range of industry-aligned skill development programs designed to empower individuals for successful careers. Our courses are crafted by experts to meet current market demands, combining practical training with placement support. Whether you are looking to enhance your technical abilities, improve your soft skills, or pursue a new professional path, our programs provide the knowledge and experience you need to excel in today’s competitive environment.

Our Courses

Apparel Made-ups and Home Furnishing

This course covers the essentials of garment manufacturing, textile design, and home furnishing production. Students learn about fabric selection, pattern making, stitching, quality control, and the latest trends in home décor. The program emphasizes practical skills, industry standards, and sustainable practices, preparing learners for roles in apparel production units, export houses, and design studios.

Beauty and Wellness

Focused on grooming, skincare, haircare, and holistic wellness, this course equips students with the knowledge and techniques required in salons, spas, and wellness centers. Topics include cosmetology, makeup artistry, therapeutic treatments, hygiene, and customer service, ensuring graduates are ready for rewarding careers in the beauty and wellness industry.

Construction

The construction course provides hands-on training in building techniques, safety protocols, project management, and the use of modern construction materials and equipment. Learners gain expertise in masonry, carpentry, plumbing, electrical work, and site supervision, making them valuable assets in the infrastructure and real estate sectors.

Sports

This program is designed for those passionate about sports management, coaching, fitness training, and event organization. It covers sports science, nutrition, injury prevention, and the business aspects of sports, preparing students for roles in sports academies, fitness centers, and sports event management companies.

Handicraft & Carpet

Explore the rich heritage of Indian handicrafts and carpet weaving. This course teaches traditional and contemporary techniques in crafting, dyeing, weaving, and finishing. Students develop artistic skills and entrepreneurial abilities, enabling them to contribute to the preservation and promotion of indigenous crafts.

Retail

The retail course covers customer service, sales techniques, inventory management, merchandising, and retail operations. Learners gain practical experience through simulations and internships, preparing them for dynamic roles in supermarkets, malls, and retail chains.

Banking and Accounting

This course imparts foundational and advanced knowledge in banking operations, financial accounting, taxation, auditing, and digital banking. Students are trained in the use of accounting software and financial analysis, making them job-ready for positions in banks, financial institutions, and corporate finance departments.

Healthcare

The healthcare course prepares students for roles in hospitals, clinics, and community health centers. It covers patient care, medical terminology, first aid, health and safety, and basic nursing skills, ensuring graduates can contribute effectively to the healthcare sector.

Electrical

This program focuses on electrical installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting. Students learn about wiring, circuit design, safety standards, and the use of electrical tools and equipment, preparing them for careers as electricians, maintenance technicians, and supervisors.

Electronic

Covering the fundamentals of electronics, this course includes training in circuit assembly, testing, repair, and the use of electronic devices. Graduates are equipped for roles in electronics manufacturing, servicing, and quality control.

Green Jobs

The green jobs course is tailored for those interested in sustainable development, renewable energy, and environmental conservation. Topics include solar and wind energy, waste management, water conservation, and eco-friendly practices, opening up opportunities in the growing green economy.

Tourism & Hospitality

This course provides comprehensive training in travel management, hotel operations, customer service, and event planning. Students gain practical skills through internships and industry projects, preparing them for successful careers in tourism, hotels, and hospitality management.
